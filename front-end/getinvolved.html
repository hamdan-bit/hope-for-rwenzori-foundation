FAQ

Common questions

Have questions about donating or volunteering? Here are the answers to the most common ones.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes. Hope for Rwenzori Foundation is a registered NGO in Uganda, and we can provide receipts for tax purposes. If you donate through our international partners, additional tax benefits may apply in your country.

What percentage goes to programs?

Over 88% of every donation goes directly to program delivery. The remaining 12% covers essential operational costs like transport, communication, and compliance. We publish full financial reports annually.

Do I need experience to volunteer?

Some roles require professional qualifications (medical, teaching), but many do not. We value enthusiasm, cultural sensitivity, and a willingness to learn. All volunteers receive an orientation before starting.

Where will I stay as a volunteer?

We arrange safe, comfortable homestays or guesthouse accommodation in Kasese town. Volunteers are responsible for their own travel to Uganda, but we handle all local logistics once you arrive.
