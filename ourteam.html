Meet the Team

Dedicated professionals and volunteers who bring passion, expertise, and local knowledge to every program we run.

Leadership

The visionaries behind our mission

Our leadership team combines decades of experience in community development, public health, and education with deep roots in the Rwenzori region.

Director

Muhindo Benard

Born and raised in Kasese District, Muhindo founded HRFR in 2016 with $500 and a borrowed classroom. He holds a degree in Community Development and has dedicated his life to grassroots social change.

Co-Director

Atugonza Joel Timothy

Atugonza oversees all program design and implementation. With 8 years in rural development, he ensures every initiative is community-led and measurable. He holds an MA in Development Studies.

Programs Lead

Olyema John Kevin

A public health specialist with a passion for rural medicine, Olyema John Kevin designed our mobile clinic model that now serves 500+ patients annually across 15 villages.

Program Team

The people making it happen

Our program coordinators work directly in communities, managing education, health, and economic empowerment initiatives on the ground.

Chief Finance Officer

Asiimwe Monica

Asiimwe manages our scholarship program and school partnerships. A former teacher himself, he has placed over 1,200 students in schools since joining in 2019.

Assistant Finance Officer

Lenny Sabina Nafuna

Olyema coordinates logistics for all three program areas, from mobile clinic routes to school supply deliveries. His knowledge of local terrain is unmatched.

Communications

Nahabwe Trust Justin

Lenny leads our women's savings groups and agricultural training programs. Under her leadership, 24 savings groups have collectively saved over $30,000.
