Our Story

From a single classroom in 2016 to a movement transforming 5 communities across the Rwenzori highlands.

Who We Are

Rooted in Rwenzori.
Built on hope.

Hope for Rwenzori Foundation is a community-driven NGO based in Kasese District, Uganda. Founded in 2016 by Muhindo Benard, we began with a simple belief: that every child deserves an education, every family deserves healthcare, and every community deserves the chance to thrive.

Today, our team of 18 full-time staff and over 20 volunteers work across 5 villages in the Rwenzori region, reaching more than 90+ beneficiaries through education, health, and economic empowerment programs.

Our Values

What we stand for

Our work is guided by six core principles that shape every program, partnership, and decision we make.

Community First

We listen before we act. Every program is designed alongside the communities we serve, ensuring solutions are locally owned and culturally grounded.

Equity & Inclusion

We prioritize the most marginalized — girls, women, people with disabilities, and remote villages — ensuring no one is left behind.

Sustainability

We build systems, not dependencies. Our programs are designed to outlast our presence, creating lasting generational change.

Accountability

Every dollar is tracked, every outcome is measured. We publish annual reports and welcome community audits of our work.

Innovation

From mobile health clinics to digital literacy labs, we embrace creative solutions that work in challenging mountain terrain.

Compassion

Behind every statistic is a human story. We lead with empathy, treating every beneficiary with the dignity they deserve.

Our Journey

Milestones that matter

From a grassroots idea to a regional force for good — our journey has been shaped by persistence, partnership, and the unwavering support of our community.

2022

The Beginning

Founded by Muhindo Benard, an idea shared with Engineering student then Asingya Collins. The idea initially was toconnect students in central region of Uganda in order to create inspiration for the younger generationto persue their studies as a way of promoting education in the Rwenzori region.

2023

Rwenzori Connect Savings scheme

The students that subscribed to the Association then called Rwenzori Connect started up a savings scheme which was to help students in need to meet some of their needs by taking loans from the savings scheme.

2024

Economic Empowerment

Introduced women's savings groups and agricultural training. 50 families received seed capital and farming education, doubling their harvest yields.

2025

Scaling Impact

Expanded to 15 communities with 12 mobile clinic units. Scholarship program grew to 300 students, and our team reached 18 full-time staff members.

2026

2,500+ Lives Changed

Today we serve over 2,500 beneficiaries annually across education, health, and economic programs — with plans to expand to the neighboring Bundibugyo district.
