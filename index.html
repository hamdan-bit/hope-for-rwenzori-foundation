Our Mission

Hope for Rwenzori Foundation provides education, health, and economic empowerment to communities across Uganda's Rwenzori region.

Founded in 2020 with just $500 and an unwavering belief that communities deserve dignity, opportunity, and care — we have grown to serve over 90 people across 2 communities.
