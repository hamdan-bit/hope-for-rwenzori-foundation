Education

Every child
deserves a future.

Our education program removes the barriers that keep children out of school. We provide scholarships, build classrooms, train teachers, and introduce digital literacy to prepare students for the modern world.

20+ Students Reached
85% Completion Rate
5 Partner Schools

Scholarships

Full and partial scholarships covering tuition, uniforms, books, and supplies for primary and secondary students.

School Infrastructure

Construction and renovation of classrooms, libraries, and sanitation facilities in underserved schools.

Digital Literacy

Computer labs and coding workshops introducing students to technology, problem-solving, and digital skills.

Health

Healthcare that
comes to you.

In the Rwenzori mountains, the nearest hospital can be a day's journey away. Our 12 mobile clinic units bring essential healthcare directly to remote villages — maternal care, vaccinations, malaria treatment, and health education.

50+ Patients Annually
12 Mobile Units
15 Villages Covered

Maternal Health

Prenatal checkups, safe delivery kits, and postnatal care for mothers in remote communities with no clinic access.

Vaccinations

Routine immunization programs for children, plus COVID-19 and seasonal flu vaccination drives.

Disease Prevention

Malaria testing and treatment, bed net distribution, and community health worker training.

Economic Empowerment

Breaking the cycle
of poverty.

Economic empowerment is about more than income — it's about dignity, choice, and the ability to build a better future. We provide training, capital, and market connections that help families thrive.

20+ Families Supported
2 Women's Groups
3x Avg Yield Increase

Agricultural Training

Modern farming techniques, seed diversification, and climate-resilient practices that triple crop yields.

Microfinance

Small loans and savings group formation that enable women to start businesses and build financial security.

Market Linkages

Connecting farmers and artisans to buyers, cooperatives, and fair-trade markets for better prices.
